```python
def plot_img(img):
    im = img
    h,w = im.shape
    plt.figure(figsize=(10,10))
    plt.imshow(im, cmap='gray')
    for y in range(h):
        for x in range(w):
            plt.annotate(str(im[y,x]), (x,y), 
                         horizontalalignment = 'center',
                         verticalalignment = 'center',
                         color= 'black' if im[y,x] > 128 else 'white')
```
